Our Story

A Family Tradition

👵🏽

"Ang pagkain ay pagmamahal"

Food is love

Since 1987, Kusina ni Lola has been serving the heartwarming flavors of the Philippines. What began as Lola Carmen's small carinderia has blossomed into a beloved gathering place where three generations of family recipes come alive.

Every dish is prepared with the same care and attention Lola taught us — from hand-ground spices to slow-simmered broths. We invite you to sit at our table and taste the love in every bite.
